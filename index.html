A Simple DSL Outage Logger

About Honest.

Honest. was developed in order to track DSL outages within a single property, to be passed onto the user's ISP.

This is not intended to troubleshoot the entire scope of issue that may cause an outage, including (but not limited to) faulty routers, DNS Lookup issues, and exceeded data allowance.

If you are using this utility to record issues with the NBN network, you may also be interested in using MrMac's (Unofficial) NBN Speed Lookup to determine what kind of connection you have to your premises.
